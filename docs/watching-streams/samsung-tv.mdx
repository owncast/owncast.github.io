    <details>
      <summary>Show step-by-step instructions</summary>

      1. Ensure your Samsung TV and device are on the same Wi-Fi network.
      2. On your phone or computer, open the Owncast stream.
      3. Use screen mirroring features:
         - **Android**: Use Smart View or Screen Mirroring
         - **iOS**: Use AirPlay (if supported by TV)
         - **Windows**: Use Project/Cast to Device
      4. Select your Samsung TV from the list of available devices.

    </details>
